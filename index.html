Xid is a social game centered around secret antagonists and an unforgiving environment. Players must work together and coordinate efforts to overcome their foes and randomly-generated (and progressively more numerous) hazards whilst simultaneously pursuing ship-wide goals such as repairing the severely-damaged warp engines and finding (or preventing) the cure to Xid, the parasitic insect infestation that threatens all of humanity.

A diverse cast of characters offer a wide range of skills and specialties that must be used thoughtfully if you are to make it home alive.

Features

  • unique multiplayer, social, roleplaying, cooperative gameplay
  • a cast of 18 diverse characters to play
  • mobile friendly, mobile first
  • retro pixel art graphics
  • procedural music soundtrack
  • active and friendly community
